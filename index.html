    We offered

    Orthomosaic

    Single 2D top view georeferenced and scaled image of the site is generated from drone data.

    Digital Surface Model

    Digital Surface Model (DSM) represents elevation of the surface with all objects on DSM for example, trees, buildings, solar and other structures and elevation of the terrain..

    Digital Terrain Model

    A DTM created from drone data provides a detailed view of the Earth's surface. High-resolution aerial imagery and advanced photogrammetry produce DTMs that represents the elevation of the terrain.

    Slope

    Slope map generated using drone data and that illustrates the steepness or degree of incline of terrain across the area. It helps to understanding and managing landscapes, offering valuable insight.

    3D Point Cloud

    A 3D point cloud generated using drone data is a detailed, three-dimensional representation of a terrain or structure created by capturing millions of individual data points from aerial surveys.

    3D Model

    A 3D model generated using drone data is a sophisticated digital representation of physical spaces. These models provide a comprehensive view of terrain or structures, offering valuable insights across various industries.

    Water Flow Analysis

    Analyze rainwater flow provides valuable insights into how water moves across a landscape to identifying potential water accumulation areas and planning effective drainage systems to manage water efficiently and mitigate related issues.

    Grid (Ground Level)

    A spot elevation on a map denotes the height of a specific point above mean sea level. It provides the exact elevation of a location. This information is crucial for planning and designing, as it helps in creating accurate 3D models and making informed decisions..

    Contour

    Contour provides a detailed and accurate representation of terrain elevations through contour lines. By capturing high-resolution elevation data and generating contour maps, drones offer essential insights for planning, design, and analysis across multiple fields.
